Travel

Thailand

This country is a truly beautiful place. It was my first time out of the US as an adult! My boyfriend and I stayed in hostels and enjoyed night markets, beaches, temples, and my favorite part- seeing the elephants. I learned a lot about myself, about my relationship and how to not take things at home for granted. The people are so respectful regardless about their feelings toward you and I was blown away by the peacefulness of the Thai. To have only been there for even just two weeks was a blessing.

Europe

In Europe, we visited Berlin, Prague, Vienna, and Frankfurt. We saw the Danube River, were awestruck by graffiti art, and fell in love with the architecture. In Prague, we had the coolest experience- it was so authentic. We met a local who took us to a festival where we got to watch two bands that paved the way for punk rock in what was formerly Czechoslovakia! In the last picture is us with the lead singer from E!E (Eh-Eh).

Random

My friends and I enjoy going to the San Juan Islands and at least one other place each year. I believe you have to make time for a variety of things in life though! Fun fact, before skydiving I had not a nervous bone in my body- I just jumped. The view was magical. Laughter is a wonderful source of happiness and Tom Segura delivers those every time. Disneyland is really the happiest place on earth. The best advice? Get there as soon as it opens, stay till it closes, eat few churros, lunch at the Blue Lagoon, and buy some ears!

Culinary

Food

My heart belongs on a plate- it’s the one true way I know how to love my people! I enjoy bringing them together with a homemade dinner- Everybody needs to eat so might as well make an event out of it, right? I try to cook with fresh ingredients and go so far as to make my own sauces, grind my own meat, and use healthier substitutes if available.

Pasta is my favorite dish to make from scratch. I finally perfected the ravioli (center). I tasted my first ever Eggs Benedict after I made it (top right). I had never tried one before and was inspired by a Masterchef challenge. It turned out delicious- crab and sweet potato bread makes all the difference. I really enjoy the challenge of some dishes and the freedom there is to make them your own.
