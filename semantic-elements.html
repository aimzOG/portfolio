Jumping into Lesson 01- What is a semantic element?

To be semantic is to have meaning. <div> itself is not semantic because it’s not descriptive- what contents is it holding? Is it a heading? A footer? A section? We don’t know just by reading <div> and would have to give each one a class. So, HTML5 has made it easier for both the browser and developer to understand what the <div> is by naming it according to what would normally be the given class.
