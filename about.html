In My Career

My first job was at the age of 18 at the YMCA working with children. I thought I was going to be a teacher- it wasn't for me. I crossed over into the restaurant world working as host and server at three restaurants over the next 3 years. That too wasn't for me. Once I began working as an office at Heptio, a tech start-up, I finally began to excell. At 25 my career is still being made and I am ecstatic to continue growing.

As a Student

My time at Heptio has lead me down to the path of project managemnet and coding.I began classes at Edmonds Community College (EdCC) for a project management certificate in summer 2018. I will recieve my certificate in March 2019. As of January 2019, I am in the front-end web development certificate program at University of Washington (UW). I am ecstatic to continue this journey and see where it leads me.
